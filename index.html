Furniture Restoration Website Plan

Site Name

Restored Elegance

This name was chosen because it represents the core business focus: restoring furniture to its elegant, original beauty. It highlights the quality and attention to detail that defines the services.

Optional domain availability: restoredelegance.com

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide information about expert furniture restoration services, including before-and-after project galleries, service descriptions, customer testimonials, and easy contact options for consultations and bookings.

Scenarios

  • Where can I view examples of furniture restoration projects?
  • How do I contact the business to schedule a restoration?

Color Schema

  • Dark Brown (#5a4d41): For all headings (h1, h2) and important text elements to convey warmth and craftsmanship.
  • Light Beige (#d9cbb6): Used as the page background to create a soft, inviting feel.
  • Medium Brown (#a67c52): Used for buttons, links, and accents to complement the dark brown.
  • Dark Gray (#333333): For all paragraph text for readability.

Typography

  • Merriweather (serif): Applied to all headings (h1, h2) to evoke elegance and tradition.
  • Roboto (sans-serif): Applied to body paragraphs and lists for readability and a modern feel.
